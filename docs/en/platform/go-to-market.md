# Souverix Platform Overview

Souverix is a sovereign signaling platform built in modern Golang for carrier and defense-grade environments.

## What Souverix Delivers

- **Border-secure interconnect** (Rempart) - Fortified SIG-GW with topology hiding and STIR enforcement
- **Intelligent IMS control** (Coeur) - High-performance signaling brain with AI integration
- **AI-native adaptive defense** (Vigie) - Real-time fraud detection and anomaly mitigation
- **Sovereign cryptographic authority** (Autorite) - PKI/HSM/Vault integration for trust sovereignty
- **Regulatory-grade intercept & emergency control** (Mandat & Priorite) - Lawful intercept and emergency services
- **Cloud-native CNF deployment** - Kubernetes/OpenShift ready, horizontal scaling
- **Active/active high resilience** - 99.999% availability, zero-downtime operations

## Target Markets

Souverix is engineered for:

- **Nations** requiring sovereign signaling control
- **Carriers** needing border-secure interconnect
- **Defense infrastructures** demanding military-grade resilience
- **Operators** seeking AI-native adaptive defense
- **Regulated industries** requiring compliance-grade intercept and emergency services

## Value Proposition

Souverix provides sovereign signaling authority for organizations that cannot compromise on:

- Security
- Resilience
- Regulatory compliance
- Cryptographic independence
- Border control
- Emergency continuity
